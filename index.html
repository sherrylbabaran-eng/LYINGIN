About Us

The Lying-In Platform is a web-based integrated management system designed to support lying-in clinics in delivering safe, efficient, and quality maternal healthcare. Our platform simplifies clinic operations while enhancing patient care through digital records, smart decision support, and mobile accessibility. We aim to bridge technology and healthcare by providing a centralized system for managing patient information, prenatal and postnatal services, appointments, and clinical reports. By using data-driven tools, the platform helps midwives and clinic staff make informed decisions that improve maternal and newborn outcomes. Developed with the needs of local lying-in clinics in mind, the Lying-In Platform promotes efficiency, accuracy, and accessibility. It empowers healthcare providers and patients alike by making essential services more organized, reliable, and available anytime, anywhere.

Clinic & Patient Management

A centralized system for managing patient records, prenatal checkups, delivery history, and postnatal care. Clinics can securely store and access patient information, schedule appointments, and track mother and baby health progress in real time.

Decision Support & Reports

Provides data-driven insights to support clinic decisions. The platform generates automated reports, health statistics, and alerts to help midwives and clinic staff identify risks, monitor outcomes, and improve quality of care.

Mobile Access & Online Services

Allows patients and staff to access services anytime using mobile devices. Features include online appointment booking, notifications for checkups and immunizations, and easy communication between patients and lying-in clinics.
